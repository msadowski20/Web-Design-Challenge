Cloudiness vs. Latitude

This graph looks at the relationship between the cloudiness of a city and its proximity to the equator (latitude 0). It shows that there does not seem to be much of a correlation between latitude and cloudiness.

We also compared the Northern and Southern Hemispheres and performed a linear regression on the findings. There does not seem to be much correlation between cloudiness and latitude when you look at the plots. However, when the linear regression is performed, you can see that there is a slight positive correlation in the Northern Hemisphere but a strong negative correlation in the Southern Hemisphere. In the Southern Hemisphere it shows that the further you are from the equator the less cloudy it is.
